Building a networked FPS in the browser

A diary of Chris’s trials and tribulations as he builds an in-browser, multiplayer first person shooter using only his scrappy can-do attitude, rudimentary knowledge of the first year of engineering calculus, and extensive googling. Thrill as you watch him make blatantly false assertions; laugh as his assertions come tumbling down in the face of reality; cry at the sheer melodrama of his writing.

Jump right in.

Chris Dickinson is a hacker living in Lawrence, KS.

He enjoys drawing, coffee, Starcraft, and chatting about JavaScript.

You can follow him on twitter or on github. He’d very much appreciate it!
